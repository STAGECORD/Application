Settings

Preferences that affect how STAGECORD looks and behaves for you.

Account

Your login email and what kind of account you have. Profile details like name, bio, photo and disciplines live on Profile.

Tied to your login. Contact hello@stagecord.com to change it.
Artists get Tracks, Projects, Events and the disciplines selector on Profile. Fans see the social feed and can follow artists.

Display

Choose your preferred currency. Project rates, commercial pricing and any payouts you see will be converted to this currency.

All prices on the platform are kept in DKK and converted for display. Updating this only changes what you see — it doesn't move any money.
