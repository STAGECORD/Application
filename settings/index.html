Settings

Preferences that affect how STAGECORD looks and behaves for you.

Account

Your login email and what kind of account you have. Profile details like name, bio, photo and disciplines live on Profile.

Tied to your login. Contact hello@stagecord.com to change it.
Artists get Tracks, Projects, Events and the disciplines selector on Profile. Fans see the social feed and can follow artists.

Display

Choose your preferred currency. Project rates, commercial pricing and any payouts you see will be converted to this currency.

All prices on the platform are kept in DKK and converted for display. Updating this only changes what you see — it doesn't move any money.

Password

Choose a new password. Minimum 8 characters. You stay logged in on this device after changing.

Notifications

Choose which events trigger an email to you. In-app notifications (bell + sidebar badges) are always shown regardless.

Privacy

Control who can see your profile and contact you on STAGECORD.

Danger zone

Permanently delete your STAGECORD account. Your account is scheduled for deletion 30 days after you confirm; you can cancel any time before then.

Delete your STAGECORD account

Before you proceed, please read carefully:

  • You will lose access to all your tracks, projects, playlists, posts and uploaded documents. Download anything you want to keep before confirming.
  • You have 30 days to cancel the deletion from this page. After that, everything is permanently removed.
  • If you regret the deletion outside the 30-day grace window, your account cannot be restored — not by you, not by us. You will need to sign up again as a new user.
  • Your username, posts, comments and reactions will disappear from other people's feeds.
