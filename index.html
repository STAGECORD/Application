Rights and licensing
for sound, dance
and creative work.

STAGECORD is where creators keep their catalogue and their terms in one place — and let the people who want to use the work license it directly. Whether you make music, choreography, performance, score or voice, your rights stay with you and every deal closes between you and the buyer.

  • Creators — register your catalogue, set your terms, license your work directly
  • Buyers — venues, filmmakers, advertisers and brands license straight from the source
  • Audiences — follow the people you love and watch their work, live or on stream

A few things people ask

When does the Beta open?

We're letting people in by role across 2026. Artists first, then general users / fans. Joining the waitlist puts you in that queue.

Is it free?

Beta access is free for everyone on the list. When the platform opens commercially, fans and artists will keep a free tier.

What happens to my invite if I don't use it?

Invites are valid for 14 days from issue. After that we recycle them back into the waitlist so others can get in.

Can I sign up as something other than Artist or Fan?

Right now we're only opening the waitlist for artists and general users. Manager, label, venue and licensor accounts come in the next wave.
