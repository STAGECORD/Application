A few things people ask

When does the Beta open?

We're letting creators in by discipline across 2026 — pick what you make on the waitlist and we'll send invites as we roll out access to that wave.

Is it free?

Beta access is free for everyone on the list. When the platform opens commercially, creators will keep a free tier.

What happens to my invite if I don't use it?

Invites are valid for 14 days from issue. After that we recycle them back into the waitlist so others can get in.

Can I sign up as a venue, label or buyer?

Not yet. Right now we're only opening the waitlist for creators. Manager, label, venue and licensor accounts come in the next wave.
