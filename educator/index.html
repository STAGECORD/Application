About

I have been producing professionally since 2014, with credits on records by Anchi Humifuku, Jeremy Freedom, and a handful of indie acts that have come through Tomorrow Records. For the last five years I have also been teaching one-on-one — both in my home studio in Vesterbro and online over Logic Pro X screen-share.

My students range from total beginners getting their first beats out, to working artists tightening up their mixes before release. Lessons are practical and project-based — we work on YOUR track, not exercises in isolation.

I assign exercise videos between lessons so you can keep building skills on your own time. You will see assigned material under "My practice plan" on your dashboard.
