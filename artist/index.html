Bio

Meet Jack "Jokesmith" Johnson, the quick-witted American comedian whose infectious humor has left audiences roaring with laughter from coast to coast. Born and raised in the bustling streets of Brooklyn, New York, Jack discovered his comedic talent at a young age, using humor as a coping mechanism to navigate life's challenges. Jack's comedic style is as diverse as the city that raised him. With a blend of observational humor, sharp wit, and a touch of self-deprecation, he effortlessly connects with audiences from all walks of life. His unique ability to find humor in the mundane and turn everyday situations into comedic gold sets him apart in the world of stand-up comedy.     2023.06.12 — Jokesmith Johnson (REGREDS)

    TRUE STORY!! So there I was, minding my own business, trying to get to my gig, when suddenly, my stomach starts rumbling like a freight train. I'm talking about seismic activity here, folks! Now, I tried to play it cool, you know, act like everything's fine. But deep down, I knew trouble was brewing. ohh lord help me out!! As the train chugged along, my stomach decides it's had enough of this underground adventure and starts making noises louder than the subway itself! People are looking at me like I've got a live chicken in my pants or something! And then, just when I think things can't get any worse, the metro comes to a screeching halt — we're stuck between stations! Now, let me tell you, being stuck in a metal tube with a gassy stomach is like being trapped in a comedy sketch written by the universe itself. But hey, when life hands you lemons, you make fart jokes, am I right? So there I am, regaling my fellow passengers with tales of my digestive distress, turning that subway car into a comedy club faster than you can say 'stand-up special.' By the time we finally reach our destination, I've got the whole train car in stitches, and let's just say the air was a little lighter — in more ways than one!

    We're backstage, right before showtime, and I'm trying to make small talk with the host. I ask, "So, what's it like hosting SNL?" And then he replies, "Oh, it's a breeze. Just like riding a bike, except the bike is on fire, and you're in hell." Needless to say, I'm sweating bullets trying to come up with a witty comeback. "You're alright, kid," they said. "You'll fit in just fine." So, no hard feelings, I'll just remember, next time I'll bring my A-game… and maybe a fire extinguisher!

    How does payment work?
    • Each member is charged their share automatically when you confirm.
    • If a member's account can't be charged, their ticket is placed on reservation — only when there's more than 2 weeks until the event.
    • Reserved members have 3 days to complete payment before the seat is released.
    • For a 2-person entourage, if one member fails to pay, both tickets are cancelled so no one attends alone.
