About

Where Music and Culture Collide in Copenhagen

Nestled in the heart of Copenhagen's vibrant Nørrebro district, RUST stands as an iconic symbol of creativity, community, and cultural diversity. Since its inception, this legendary venue has served as a hub for emerging artists, seasoned performers, and passionate music enthusiasts alike. Originally founded in 1989, RUST has undergone several transformations throughout the years, evolving from a humble student-run café to a dynamic multi-purpose space that encompasses a concert hall, nightclub, and cultural center. Its rich history and unwavering commitment to fostering artistic expression have solidified its status as a cornerstone of Copenhagen's live music scene. At RUST, music knows no boundaries. From indie rock to electronic beats, hip-hop to folk, the venue boasts a diverse lineup of local and international acts, providing a platform for artists from all walks of life to showcase their talent and connect with audiences on a deeper level.…

MIKE is an alt-rap mainstay. The 24-year-old artist's evasion is evident in both his calm demeanor and measured exposition. Uncompromising and with a true artistic vision — 20-year-old Lola Young has the potential, spirit and individuality to become Britain's and Europe's next great talent. With 31,000 monthly listeners on Spotify alone, Mellow storms forward as a breath of fresh air to the established Danish music scene.
