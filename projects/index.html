🎼Topliner production hints

From Maya Thompson · topliner
  • Vocal style Push the falsetto on the second chorus — drops the dynamic floor before the bridge hits.
  • Song structure Try a half-time feel on the pre-chorus so the chorus lift feels twice as big in contrast.
  • Tempo & feel Sidde i 92 BPM i stedet for 96 — vocal-phrasing får mere room at trække vejret.
  • Production Drop the snare on bar 17-20 of the bridge — let the vocal carry alone for that resolution moment.
